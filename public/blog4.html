The Impact of Graphic Design on Company Branding and Identity

With approximately 90% of information processed by the brain being visual, the importance of high-quality graphic design services in marketing strategies cannot be overstated. Visual content is pivotal for over 75% of marketing efforts, making graphic design a key player in branding success. As demand for skilled graphic designers rises, companies are increasingly turning to graphic design courses to fill the talent gap effectively.
